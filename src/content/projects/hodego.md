## What it does

Hodego is a platform that connects athletes with world-class coaches and sports experts for personalized training, remote guidance, and in-competition advice from people who've competed at the highest levels of the sport.

## Why it matters

The craft of elite coaching has always been locked behind proximity — you had to know the right person, live in the right city, afford the right club. Hodego breaks that constraint open with remote access, on-demand sessions, and live competition support. An enormous market of serious recreational athletes has never had a way in; we're building it.

Co-founder. I work across product, data, and the accounting side of the operation — the trilingual role a company at this stage actually needs.
